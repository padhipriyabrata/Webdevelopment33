What Is ezTruck?

ezTruck is Odisha's cutting-edge technology-based Logistics services provider. Where you can book trucks online, with only a few clicks.

Lower Transit Times

Relay-led operations lead to a reduction in transit times by 50-70% as compared to conventional transport means. This empowers our customers to reimagine their logistics and supply chain requirements.

First Time In Odisha

ezTruck provides innovative service solutions to individuals & businesses in cities and remote corners of Odisha. We were among the first in the mining logistics business to implement technology.

Safety

All our vehicles are containerized hence leading to safe and secure transit of goods for all industries. In addition to that, we have advanced locking mechanisms to ensure the safety of consignments.

Service and Reliability

We have one of the largest owned fleets in Odisha of over 1000 vehicles enabling us to handle both voluminous and dense cargo. We have scored 95%+ on reliability for placement assurance from our clients.

Logistics Service

Our innovative culture provides online truck booking services like local logistics, packers, and movers, mining, and other services in Bhubaneswar and Cuttack.
